What does a smart shoe do? Smart shoes come equipped with battery-operated systems that require charging before use. They can be connected to other devices (e.g. smartphones) typically via Bluetooth. Insoles can be equipped with a variety of sensors such as a gyroscope, accelerometer, pressure sensor, altimeter, GPS, etc.
